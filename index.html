Welcome to the Maze!

In this story, you will make choices to progress along the paths. If you wish to stop at any point, type "stop" to end the game.

How to Play

1. Follow the prompts on the screen.

2. Enter your responses in the input field.

3. Make choices and progress through the story.

4. Have fun and enjoy the adventure!
